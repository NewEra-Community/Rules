Server Rules

 

  1. Don't Ping Owner/Devs/Respect
    You can ping Helper for help!

  2. Be respectful
    This means no mean, rude, or harassing comments. Treat others the way you want to be treated.

  3. No inappropriate language
    Use of profanity should be kept to a reasonable minimum. Any derogatory language towards any user is prohibited. Swearing is permitted in casual channels only, while the official help and code channels should be kept free of any profane language.

  4. No spamming
    Do not send a lot of small messages right after each other. These disrupt chat and make it hard to scroll through the server. Please keep your messages at least 5 words long while chatting.

  5. No pornographic/adult/other NSFW material
    The idea behind the server is to provide a safe place for us to share art, videos, and other kinds of creative material – not to share the aforementioned NSFW material.

  6. No advertisements
    No invasive advertising, whether it be for other communities or streams. You can post your content in the media channel if it’s relevant and provides actual value for the community.

  7. No offensive names and profile pictures
    Keep your names and profile picture appropriate.

  8. Server Raiding
    Server raiding is against the Discord terms of service. Any attempt to circumvent or bypass them can result in a permanent ban.

  9. Threats are forbidden
    Threats are prohibited and disallowed.

  10. Follow the Discord Community Guidelines
    Respect the general Discord Community guidelines.

  11. Do not join voice chat channels without permission of the people already in there
    If you see that they have a free spot it is alright to join and ask whether they have an open spot, but leave if your presence is not wanted by whoever was there first.

  12. Don’t share your personal information
    Do not share your personal information or the personal information of other users without their consent. This includes phone numbers, addresses, and any other sensitive information.

  13. Don’t share  illegal or pirated content  
    Do not share links to illegal or pirated content, such as copyrighted material or stolen software. This is against the Discord terms of service and can result in a ban from the server.

  14. Avoid false or misleading information
    Make sure to fact-check any information that you share and only share reliable sources. Spreading false or misleading information can be harmful and undermines the trust and integrity of the community.

  15.  Follow the channel-specific guidelines
    Each channel in your server should have specific guidelines for the type of content that is allowed. Make sure to follow these guidelines and respect the purpose of each channel.

  16. No sharing or distributing unauthorized tools or services
    Sharing or distributing unauthorized tools or services is strictly prohibited on the server.

  17.  No sharing or distributing viruses or malicious software  
    Sharing or distributing viruses or malicious software can harm the server and its members, and is therefore strictly prohibited.

  18. No sharing or distributing illegal drugs or controlled substances
    distributing illegal drugs or controlled substances is strictly prohibited on the server.

  19. No sharing or distributing counterfeit or fraudulent items
    Sharing or distributing counterfeit or fraudulent items, such as fake IDs or fraudulent financial documents, is strictly prohibited on the server.

  20. No sharing or distributing stolen financial or banking information
    Distributing stolen financial or banking information, such as credit card numbers or bank account information, is strictly prohibited.

  21.  No sharing or distributing unauthorized keys or serial numbers for software or games
    It is strictly prohibited to share or distribute keys or serial numbers for software or games that have been obtained illegally.

  22. Respect others’ privacy
    Do not invade the privacy of other users by asking for or  sharing their personal information, such as real names, addresses, or social media accounts without their consent.

  23. Do not impersonate other users, staff members, or any other individuals or organizations
    This includes using similar usernames, profile pictures, or display names.

  24. No excessive self-promotion
    Share your work in moderation and only in designated channels.

  25. Keep political and religious discussions civil and respectful
    Or avoid them altogether if they are not relevant to the server’s purpose.

  26. Do not intentionally disrupt conversations or create a hostile environment for other users
    This includes  trolling, flaming, or excessive arguing.

  27. No public shaming or call-outs
    If you have an issue with another user or their behavior, address it privately or report it to a staff member. Do not publicly shame or call out other users in the server.

  28. Do not abuse server bots
    Only use server bots for their intended purposes and do not spam or misuse their commands.

  29. Keep discussions relevant to the server’s topic
    Stay on topic and  ensure that your discussions are relevant to the server’s purpose  or the specific channel you are in.

  30. Do not excessively use caps or emojis in your messages
    This can be disruptive and make the chat difficult to follow.

  31. No sharing or promoting illegal activities
    Do not share or promote any illegal activities, such as hacking, phishing, or drug use.

  32. No self-destructive behavior or encouragement of self-harm
    Do not encourage, glorify, or engage in self-destructive behavior or self-harm. This includes sharing graphic content related to self-harm or discussing personal experiences in a way that may trigger others.

  33. Do not share graphic or disturbing content
    Such as gore, violence, or explicit sexual content.

  34. No promoting or discussing drugs, alcohol, or other substances
    Avoid discussing or promoting the use of drugs, alcohol, or other substances that may be harmful or illegal.

  35. No soliciting or begging for money, items, or services
    Do not solicit or beg for money, items, or services from other users.

  36. No blackmail, threats, or harassment
    Do not engage in blackmail, threats, or harassment of any kind, whether in public or private messages.

  37. No sharing of personal or private messages without consent
    Do not share the contents of personal or private messages with others without the consent of all involved parties.

  38. No excessive use of voice changer software
    If using voice changer software, do so in moderation and avoid excessive or disruptive use in voice channels.

  39. No recruiting for other servers or communities
    Do not recruit or promote other servers or communities in this server without prior approval from the server staff.

  40. Do not evade bans or mutes
    If you have been banned or muted, do not attempt to evade the punishment by creating alternate accounts or otherwise circumventing the restrictions.

  41. Keep content age-appropriate
    Ensure that all content you share is age-appropriate.


The Admins and Moderators will Mute/Kick/Ban per discretion.

Your presence in this server implies accepting these rules, including all further changes. These changes might be done at any time without notice, it is your responsibility to check for them.
